About Us

Innovation in Magnetics & Customer Partnerships

At SM Magnetics our goal is to assist customers from Design to Production, and that means being the most valued supplier in technical assistance, prototype production, mass production, and many other areas important to our customers. SM Magnetics was founded in 2014 following the growth of SuperMagnetMan, a renowned online magnet retailer for over 25 years. Through SuperMagnetMan, we offer fast prototyping of magnets and magnetic assemblies for testing.
